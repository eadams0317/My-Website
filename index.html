Flight Ops (Club) President (Fall 2021)

President of Detachment 155's aviation club: Flight Ops. Held weekly meetings and instructed over 10 peers in the fundamentals and principles of aviation. Networked with multiple Active Duty USAF pilots and coordinated Zoom call discussions/Q&As.
